This project was made for the class ECS 162 Web Programming. The theme of this arcade was sushi related services. These services include sushi delivery (delivering food to a table), sushi stack (plating sushi in a unique way), and menu match (organizing menus). The intended audience for this arcade is anyone who enjoys testing their speed on finishing games and sushi lovers!

Hey! I'm Hanson, the creator of this website.
I hope you enjoy your stay here :)

Food is great. I like a lot of different types of food, but sushi has a special place in my stomach. I've started from the humble California roll and have reached up to Uni Gunkan. With each level up, the prices soar to heights unimaginable. I've only scratched the surface of the sushi world and hope to explore more of it in the future. With the limits of my wallet, I will continue to enjoy the paradise of sushi.
